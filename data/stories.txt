#1
Welcome to Hogwarts, the school of witchcraft and wizardry! You are new to magic after discovering your magical heritage. You will now begin your journey by taking the Hogwart's Express to campus after purchasing your school supplies at Diagon Alley. You must choose who to sit next to on the train.
#2
You choose to sit next to Hermione which increases your intellect points. Her smart wit will help you in future puzzles.
#3
You choose to sit with Ron who will become your loyal friend. He makes certain challenges easier with his help.
#4
You choose to sit along and meet Neville. He becomes your loyal friend and adds unique support later in the game.
